Written by a Fleet Street veteran journalist now based in Northumberland, this blog contains the authors first-hand original interviews with the casts, production teams and writers of major British television dramas and dates back (originally) to 2006 with a move to the current platform and address in 2009. With some other content relating to their attendance at many historic news events over the last 40 years, including several royal weddings and funerals plus general elections, significant Old Bailey and High Court cases dating back to the Yorkshire Ripper trial, IRA and more recent bombings etc. An archive of journalistic material not available anywhere else and - as a working freelance journalist - still being regularly updated today.
