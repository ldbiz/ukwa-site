Research Capacity Building Collaboration (RCBC) is funded by the Welsh Government through Health and Care Research Wales and is a collaboration between six university nursing and allied health departments in Wales. The aim is to drive an increase in the research capacity and capability of nursing and allied health professional groups and to contribute toward the development of clinical academic roles.
