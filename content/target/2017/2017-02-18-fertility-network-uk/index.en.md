Fertility Network UK is a small patient-focused fertility charity which provides support services, information and access to a community of people affected by fertility problems. They campaign for equitable access to NHS fertility treatment in the UK and co-chair campaign group Fertility Fairness. Their education project raises awareness of the importance of fertility education and they work with the media to raise public awareness of all aspects of fertility issues.
