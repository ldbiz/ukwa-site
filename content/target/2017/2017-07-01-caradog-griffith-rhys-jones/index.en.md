Griffith Rhys Jones, or Caradog (1834 – 1897), was a Welsh conductor of 'Côr Mawr' (the South Wales Choral Union), which twice won first prize at The Crystal Palace choral competitions in London in 1872 and 1873. A statue of Caradog is located in Aberdare. This website was created by Friends of Caradog Society.
