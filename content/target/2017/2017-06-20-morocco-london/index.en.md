Although major Moroccan migration to the UK happened in early 1960’s, Moroccans have been living in the UK since 1800’s and Moroccan and British relations, both economic and political go back to the thirteenth century. According to official reports there are an estimated 60, 000 – 70,000 Moroccans in the UK of which 35,000 live in London, mainly in the borough of Kensington and Chelsea, Westminster, Hammersmith and Fulham, Lewisham and Hackney.  MoroccoLondon.co.uk, online since 2004 focusing on everything Moroccan in London.
