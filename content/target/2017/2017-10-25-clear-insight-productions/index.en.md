Deborah Claire Procter is the founder of Clear Insight Productions and a Fellow of the Royal Society of the Arts. Deborah Claire Procter is a multi-media artist from Wales who makes performances for theatres, galleries and site-specific spaces. She has received a Creative Wales Award allowing her to continue with video and experimental dance and two Wales Arts International awards that allowed her to visit Argentina.
