The Institute of Commonwealth Studies, founded in 1949, is the only postgraduate academic institution in the United Kingdom devoted to the study of the Commonwealth. As a member of the School of Advanced Study, established in 1994, the Institute works with eight other postgraduate research institutes to offer academic opportunities across and between a wide range of subject fields in the humanities and social sciences.
