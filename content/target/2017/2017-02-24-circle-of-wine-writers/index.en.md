The Circle of Wine Writers is an association of authors, writers, journalists, bloggers, broadcasters, photographers and lecturers, communicating about wines and spirits. Founded in 1960 by the late Cyril Ray, based in the UK, there are circa 300 members worldwide. The Circle of Wine Writers organises a programme of workshops, meetings, talks and tastings.
