The NBN Atlas Wales is a partnership between the Welsh Government and Natural Resources Wales and collates species and habitat data and 'offering functionality including the ability to view photographs, search for biological data by predefined areas, by postcode or by polygon search tools, find organisations working in a particular area (geographic or taxonomic) and create alerts for species records'. The NBN Atlas Wales holds over 12 million species occurrence records for Wales.
