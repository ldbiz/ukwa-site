Amber Rudd was elected as Conservative MP for Hastings & Rye in 2010 and relected in 2015. She subsequently stood for re-election in 2017 and was successful. She served as  Secretary of State for Energy and Climate Change from May 2015 to July 2016, when she was promoted to Home Secretary.
