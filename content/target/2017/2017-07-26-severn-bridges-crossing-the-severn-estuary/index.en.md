The website provides a history of the development of the road transport links between the South West of England and the South of Wales including the building of the two Seven Bridges. This website is a permanent public display for the exhibition material from the Visitors Centre which is now closed. The web site was published in 2016, to coincide with the 50th Anniversary of the opening of the Severn Bridge, and the 20th Anniversary of the completion of the Second Severn Crossing.
