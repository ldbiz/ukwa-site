The Species Habitat Protection Group is a voluntary charity group based in Llandinam, Powys to monitor and help protect local Barn Owls and their breeding sites, particularly in upland areas and have received a grant from the Heritage Lottery Fund for our "Barn Owls: Vision On" project.
