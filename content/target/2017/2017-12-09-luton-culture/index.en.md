About

Luton Culture is an arts and cultural charity for the people of Luton and beyond. We showcase high quality arts and cultural activity across our three inspiring heritage sites; Stockwood Discovery Centre, Wardown House, Museum and Gallery and The Hat Factory Arts Centre. We also deliver community-focused Library services and arts in the public realm engagement projects.
