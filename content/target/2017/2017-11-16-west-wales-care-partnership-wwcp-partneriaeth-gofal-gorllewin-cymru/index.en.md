West Wales Care Partnership (WWCP) oversee delivery of the Sustainable Social Services agenda and ensure that statutory requirements of the Social Services and Wellbeing (Wales) Act 2014 are met. The Partnership cover Cardiganshire, Carmarthenshire and Pembrokeshire.
