Jimmy's Farm, as seen on TV, home of the rare breed Essex Pig. They
are a working farm and are open for visitors 7 days a week. There's loads to do at the farm for a great family day out. They have a Nature Trail, Woodland Walk, Beautiful Garden's, Farm Shop, Adventure Play Area, Barn Restaurant and Field Kitchen. They sell all of their home reared free range pig produce, beef and lamb in their butchers as well as an abundance of herbs and veggies from their garden.
