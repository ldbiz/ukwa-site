St John the Baptist Church has existed for nearly a thousand years and is one of the oldest buildings in Erith.

The Christian family at St. John’s is made up of people of all ages and backgrounds.

The Parish Church of St. John the Baptist is also fondly known as St. John’s or as “The Old Church”.

Founded in the 12th Century as the ancient Parish Church of Erith, it is a Grade 11* listed building, full of interesting architectural features and tombs.
