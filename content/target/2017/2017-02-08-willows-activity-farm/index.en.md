Willows Activity Farm is a Children’s Farm just off junction 22 of the M25. They provide a day out with shows, fun fair rides, carousels, inflatables, Tractor Ride, acres of outdoor and under cover soft play activities plus entry to the Peter Rabbit Adventure Playground. They have farmyard animals in the kids’ farm, the countryside setting with delicious homemade food available in our catering outlets, gift shop, free parking and an easy to find location. They also have a programme of fantastic seasonal events including Pumpkin Festival and February Frolics with live lambing.
