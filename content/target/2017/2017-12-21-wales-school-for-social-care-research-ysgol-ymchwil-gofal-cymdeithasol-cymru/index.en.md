The Wales School for Social Care Research is based at Swansea University and was established to 'bridge the gap between academic research and social care services in Wales in collaboration, to support research knowledge mobilisation, and to support public engagement in social care research in wales' and inform improvements in the delivery of quality social care for children, young people and adults as outlined in the Social Services and Wellbeing (Wales) Act 2014 with its focus on wellbeing, people, prevention, and integration.
