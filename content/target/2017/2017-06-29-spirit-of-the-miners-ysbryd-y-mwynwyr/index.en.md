Spirit of the Miners was a community regeneration project established to establish an identity for northern Ceredigion using the legacy of metal mining as a theme for regeneration bys focusing on the human, social and community aspects of mining culture pivotal features on why the upland villages exist. The project was managed by Ceredigion County Council and funded by the Welsh Assembly Government, the European Objective 1 programme and the Countryside Council for Wales.
