Dr Alun Withey is an academic historian of medicine and the body, and 2014 AHRC/BBC 'New Generation Thinker'. His research includes the history of medicine in early modern Wales, and he is an associate research fellow on the Wellcome-funded project ‘The Medical World of England, Ireland and Wales, 1550-1715’. He is currently lecturer ar Exeter University having previously taught at Swansea University.
