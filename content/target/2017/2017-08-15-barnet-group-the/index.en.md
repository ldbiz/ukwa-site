Created by Barnet Council in 2004, they manage and maintain council homes and leasehold properties. In February 2012 they became part of The Barnet Group, a local authority trading company owned by Barnet Council. They also have a sister social care company, Your Choice Barnet, providing services to adults with a range of physical and learning disabilities.
