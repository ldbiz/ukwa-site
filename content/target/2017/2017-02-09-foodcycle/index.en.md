FoodCycle builds communities by combining volunteers, surplus food and spare kitchen spaces to create nutritious meals for people at risk of food poverty and social isolation.

Aims
Build stronger communities through shared meals
Reduce food poverty by providing nutritious, tasty three-course meals to those in need
Reduce social isolation and loneliness by providing communal dining experiences
