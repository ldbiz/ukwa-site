Eric and Jessica Huntley, founders of Bogle L'Ouverture publishers, are two ordinary people who have transformed the world around them through a shared commitment to publishing, community action and justice. huntleysonline.com is dedicated to providing information on their lives through an easily accessible website. Anyone interested in researching their lives can find a variety of entry points here to explore the significant impact ­they’ve had on the community at large and the Black community in particular. For London Metropolitan Archives, the Huntley collection is the first major collection from the African-Caribbean community in London, covering fifty years of Black history and providing an unparalleled view of the African Caribbean community in London.
