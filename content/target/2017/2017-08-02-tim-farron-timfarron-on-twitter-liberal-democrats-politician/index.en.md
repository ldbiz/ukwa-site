Tim Farron stood as Liberal Democrats candidate for Westmorland and Lonsdale at the 2015 General Election. He was first elected to serve as MP for Westmorland and Lonsdale in 2005 and re-elected in 2015. He stood for re-election again in 2017 and was successful.  He was elected as Party  leader in 2015 and resigned on 14 June 2017, remaining as leader until a successor is elected. He is a strong supporter of remaining in the EU.
