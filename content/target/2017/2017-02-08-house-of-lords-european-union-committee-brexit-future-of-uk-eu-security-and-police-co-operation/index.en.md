Maintaining the strong security cooperation the UK currently has with the
EU will be one of the Government’s top four overarching objectives in the
forthcoming negotiations on the UK’s exit from, and future relationship with,
the European Union. This report examines the main tools and institutions that currently underpin police and security cooperation between the United Kingdom and the European Union and explores the options available to the Government for retaining or replacing them when the UK leaves the EU.
