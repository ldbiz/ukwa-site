Campaign to protect Brockwell Park and the local community from the damage done by large corporate events.  "Lambeth need to raise revenue to support green spaces. Events are one way to do that. But, in their drive for funds, Lambeth are not being realistic about the scale of event Brockwell Park can handle or that the community will put up with. Brixton and Herne Hill have a strong history of music and community, and Brockwell Park is a good place for some events. The Lambeth Country Show is a good example of an event that is popular locally. But our Park shouldn't become just another venue for generic commercial gigs. Our local economy shouldn't suffer while event organisers make millions. Gig-goers shouldn't get a poor experience. Local people shouldn't be cut off from their only green space."
