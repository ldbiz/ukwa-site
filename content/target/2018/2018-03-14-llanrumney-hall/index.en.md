Llanrumney Hall is Grade II* listed building in Llanrumney, Cardiff built in 1450. The Hall has now fallen into disrepair. The Llanrumney Hall Community Trust is a community led trust established to regenerate Llanrumney Hall into a community hub to mitigate and prevent poverty in East Cardiff.
