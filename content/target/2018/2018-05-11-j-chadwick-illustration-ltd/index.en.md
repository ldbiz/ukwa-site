Jacqueline Chadwick is a professional archaeological illustrator. She creates original graphics, maps, finds-drawings, reconstructions, artwork and design for the interpretation of archaeological collections and for the publication of archaeological research and is a member of the Lithic Studies Society, the Association of Illustrators and York Consortium for Conservation and Craftsmanship.
