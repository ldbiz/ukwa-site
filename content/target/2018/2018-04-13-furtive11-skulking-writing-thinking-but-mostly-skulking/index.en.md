This is a blog by the poet and author, Meryl Pugh (1968-). She has a PhD in Critical and Creative Writing from the University of East Anglia and writes about interiority, environment and poetics. She is the of author of two pamphlets — The Bridle (Salt Publishing, 2011) and Relinquish (Arrowhead, 2007) — and a book — Natural Phenomena (Penned in the Margins Press, 2018). Meryl Pugh grew up in Wales, New Zealand, East Anglia and the Forest of Dean, where her family settled.
