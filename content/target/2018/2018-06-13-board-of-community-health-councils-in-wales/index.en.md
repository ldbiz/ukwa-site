The Community Health Council (CHC) works to enhance and improve the quality of your local health service and are statutory and independent voice in health services provided throughout Wales. The Board of Community Health Councils in Wales was established in 2004 with the aim to advise, assist and monitor Community Health Councils(CHCs) with respect to the performance and of their functions and to represent the collective views and interests of CHCs to the Welsh Ministers.
