Bank of Scotand PLC is a commercial and clearing bank based in Edinburgh, Scotland. The Governor and Company of the Bank of Scotland was established by an Act of the Parliament of Scotland in 1695, and in 2201 the Bank of Scotland and Halifax merged to form HBOS, or "Halifax Bank of Scotland", and in 2008 HBOS Group were taken over by Lloyds TSB Group.
