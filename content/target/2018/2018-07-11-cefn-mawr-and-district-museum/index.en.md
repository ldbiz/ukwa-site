The Cefn Mawr musuem is located in Wrexham and was opened in 2015. The musuem focuses on local community in Cefn Mawr and the wider area. The Museum contains various sections, each of which focuses on an aspect of community life: Village Life, Schools, Industry, Transport, the Canal and the River Dee, War Memorabilia, Churches and Chapels, Sport, Music and Art.
