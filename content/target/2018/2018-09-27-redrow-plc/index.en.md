Redrow plc is one of the UK's leading residential housing developers, our purpose is to create a better way for people to live. Established in 1974 by Steve Morgan, Redrow was a small civil engineering concern in North Wales. It is now listed in the Wales top 300 companies for 2017, a list compiled by Professor Dylan Jones-Evans of the University of South Wales.
