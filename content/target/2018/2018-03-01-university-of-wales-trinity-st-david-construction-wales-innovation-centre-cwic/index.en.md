The Construction Wales Innovation Centre (CWIC) was established in 2016 and is a CITB funded all-Wales training centre. They, along with the University of Wales Trinity Saint David, have been deliveringspecialist training to all sectors and levels within the welsh construction industry including its supply chain. Its hub forms part of the new SA1 Swansea Waterfront Innovation Quarter.
