RecordDNA is a Northumbria University and UCL led international research network bringing together academics, practitioners, users and systems developers to develop a new cross-disciplinary network of stakeholders with wide ranging expertise to explore the question ‘In the digital era what is the concept of the record and what implications are there for the usability of the future the evidence base?’ Through this work they hope to aid the maintenance of the digital evidence base through time.
