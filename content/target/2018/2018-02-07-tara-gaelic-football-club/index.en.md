Tara GFC are one of the biggest conglomerate clubs in London, Tara Gaelic Football club consists of Tara Ladies, Mens, Senior, Intermediate, Minor, under 18, under 16, under 14, under 12, under 10, and under 8s.

Founded in the 1930s, Tara is a club which has traditionally had strong links with Leitrim. Prionnsias Redican & his family are now very much involved with Grainne and Ciara involved with the ladies and underage while Andy and Diarmuid are members of the senior squad. Its members now hail from all over Ireland.
