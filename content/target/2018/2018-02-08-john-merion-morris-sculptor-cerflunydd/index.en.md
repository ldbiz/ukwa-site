John Meirion Morris is an important Welsh Sculptor and elected member of the Royal Cambrian Academy of Art (RCA) and has received the ‘Owain Glyndwr award’ – for outstanding contribution to the Arts in Wales. He is best known for his portraits, many of which are at the National Library of Wales, Aberystwyth.
