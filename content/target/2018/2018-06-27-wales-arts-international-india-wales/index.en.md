The UK/ India Year of Culture 2017 provides an opportunity to build on existing and create new relationships through the arts. This is a part of the partnership between Arts Council Wales/Wales Arts International and British Council and fund new collaborative arts projects between India and Wales. The programme will feature multiple art forms - theatre, dance, visual arts, literature, music and applied arts and craft.
