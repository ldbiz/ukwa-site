C.R Archaeology, established in 2011, provides archaeological evaluation and excavation, building recording and analysis, walkover surveys, archaeological illustration and geophysical surveys. The company has undertaken numerous high profile excavations in the North Wales area including the recent discovery of a unique Neolithic settlement site in Anglesey, excavations at Kendrick's Cave, Llandudno, Harlech Castle and works to Barclodiad y Gawres, a chambered tomb.
