Art of Football is a festival of the culture of football to coincide with the World Cup. It is running in Liverpool from the 14th June - 15th July 2018. It was commissioned by Culture Liverpool and curated by Bido Lito! Magazine in collaboration with Foto Octo and Laces Out!. This is the official Instagram account of the festival.
