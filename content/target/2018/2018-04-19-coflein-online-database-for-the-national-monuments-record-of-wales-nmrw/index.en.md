Coflein is an online catalogue of archaeology, buildings, industrial and maritime heritage in Wales. Coflein contains details of many thousands of archaeological sites, monuments, buildings and maritime sites in Wales, together with an index to the drawings, manuscripts and photographs held in the NMRW archive collections. It is  curated by the Royal Commission on the Ancient and Historical Monuments of Wales.
