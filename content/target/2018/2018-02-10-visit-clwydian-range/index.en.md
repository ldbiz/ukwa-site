This is a tourist information guide to the Clwydian Range, designated as an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty (AONB). The Range runs north-south for 25 miles from the coast at Prestatyn to the Nant y Garth Pass. The site is managed by the Clwydian Range Tourism Group.
