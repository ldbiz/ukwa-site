A fundraising event for the Together for Yes Campaign to repeal the 8th Amendment to the constitution of the Republic of Ireland. The evening includes the screening of a number of short films based around the Together for Yes Campaign, and short spoken word performances. Screenings are all original films and there will be a panel with selected directors and writers.
