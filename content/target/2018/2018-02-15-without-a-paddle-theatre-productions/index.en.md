Without A Paddle Theatre Productions is the company of Musical Theatre songwriters/librettists, Christopher J. Orton and Robert Gould. Their works include the musicals My Land's Shore, Elephant Juice, Based On A True Story, Grace Notes and Flop!, as well as the plays The Road To Port Of Barry, Independence Dai and The Shed. Christopher J. Orton is a composer/writer/ actor/singer/musician from Chepstow and Robert Gould is a graduate of the University Of Wales,  Swansea, a former teacher and author of history books for schools.
