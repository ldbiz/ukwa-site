In general, Sino-British Business Alliance (SBBA) exists to help UK SMEs who wanting to do business in China. We hold business networking event on a monthly basis in China and is currently free for UK companies to participate. We also hold seminars and networking events focus on different industries regarding the theme of China Business Link in London and Cambridge.
