According to French reports, the new "international chamber" in Paris is an attempt to capitalise on Brexit and steal London's crown as a global hub for lucrative commercial legal disputes. Other English-language courts are popping up in the Netherlands, Germany and Belgium, with those behind them also talking about seizing opportunities from the UK's departure from the EU.
