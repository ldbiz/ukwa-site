Unlocking the hidden history of women's football is a project run by the National Football Museum. The Museum will extend its permanent galleries to display its women’s collection which includes international material from the early days of the game through to recent FIFA Women’s World Cups. The new display will open to the public in March 2018 reveal more about the history of women in football.
