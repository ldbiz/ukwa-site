Alastair Duncan is a visual artist who works in digital media, including photography and sound and influenced by his interest in tactile, visual and aural texture. He moved to Wales in 1983 and established a studio in Swansea`s Maritime Quarter. He works as a professional artist/craftsman in both the commission/public art field and in education.
