The Children's Rights Wales website providesdetails of all the rights for children in the UNCRC and includes many resources, films, games and information. UNCRC stands for United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child. It's a list of children's rights that all children and young people, everywhere in the world have, no matter who they are, where they live or what they believe in.
