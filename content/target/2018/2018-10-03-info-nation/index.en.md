Info-Nation is Swansea's one-stop shop for young people and offer, information, advice and specialist support across a range of issues, to young people, aged 11-25, and their families.. The one-stop shop is a partnership between Evolve (the City & County of Swansea's Young People Services), Barnardos Cymru and Drugaid Cymru, and is located on the Kingsway, in Swansea's City Centre.
