Boshemia is brought to you by a global feminist collective based in the UK and US. Founded by a transatlantic trio—E, Q, and L—we are storytellers and activists, collaborating globally with writers, artists, and other creatives to produce both Boshemia Blog and Boshemia Magazine. "Boshemia is a place for intersectional feminists and free expression; we welcome womxn and nonbinary folk into this space with open arms. We strive to cultivate an energized and engaged community of feminists who share pop culture, politics, reviews, critiques, and personal essays."
