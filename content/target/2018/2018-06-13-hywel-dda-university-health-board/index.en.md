Hywel Dda University Health Board healthcare services covers Carmarthenshire, Ceredigion and Pembrokeshire. The Board provides Acute, Primary, Community, Mental Health and Learning Disabilities services via General and Community Hospitals, Health Centres, GPs, Dentists, Pharmacists and Optometrists and other sites.
