Culture and Cosmos is a peer-reviewed academic journal in the history of astrology and cultural astronomy published by the Sophia Centre Press in partnership with the Sophia Centre for the Study of Cosmology in Cultureat the University of Wales Trinity Saint David. Culture and Cosmos is the only peer-reviewed journal to focus on the history of astrology and cultural aspects of the history of astronomy and was founded in 1997.
