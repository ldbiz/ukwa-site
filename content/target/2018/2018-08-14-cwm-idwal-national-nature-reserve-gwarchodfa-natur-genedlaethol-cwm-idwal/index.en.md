Cwm Idwal was designated as a National Nature Reserve in 1954, primarily because of its unique geology and its rare plants that have colonised along the slopes. Cwm Idwal is 5 miles south of Bethesda and about 4 miles west of Capel Curig. This is a guide by the National Trust to the area.
