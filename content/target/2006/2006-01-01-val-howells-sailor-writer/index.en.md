Val Howells was one of five competitors in the first Observer Single-handed Transatlantic Race. He subsequently sailed around the world single-handed. Val also wrote two books about his sailing adventures: 'Sailing Into Solitude' and 'Up My Particular Creek'.
This is his personal website publicising his books. Val Howells died in 2017.
