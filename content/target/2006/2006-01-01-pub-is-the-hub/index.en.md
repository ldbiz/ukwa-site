<p>Website of the 'pub is the hub' initiative, which offers support to rural pubs and licensees on diversification of services. The aim is to ensure that community services such as post offices or shops continue to have a location in smaller communities. The initiative was launched by the Prince of Wales in 2001, and won the Prime Minister's 'Big Society' award in 2014.</p>
