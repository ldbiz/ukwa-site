Lave Net fishing is a traditional method of fishing which has been practiced for hundreds of years. The lave net fishermen at Black Rock preserve this tradition as they are the last of their kind in wales and has been past down through the generations. The fishermen all have their roots in the villages of Sudbrook, Portskewett and Caldicot in Monmouthshire.
