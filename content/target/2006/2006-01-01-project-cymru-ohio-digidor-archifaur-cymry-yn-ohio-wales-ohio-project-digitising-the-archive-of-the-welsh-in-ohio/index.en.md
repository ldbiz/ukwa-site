The goal of the Wales-Ohio Project is to digitize a selection of Welsh Americana relating to the state of Ohio held at The National Library of Wales and to make it available to audiences world-wide via this website.
This site displays more than 10,000 images of archive, manuscript and printed material, photographs and maps, the contents of The Cambrian magazine (1880-1919), sections which chronicle the history and experiences of the Welsh settlers in Ohio in the nineteenth century.
