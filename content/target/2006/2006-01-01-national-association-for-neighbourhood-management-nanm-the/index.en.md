<p>The National Association for Neighbourhood Management (NANM) is an independent not-for-profit organisation. It promotes neighbourhood management and neighbourhood action through membership and to wider audiences, delivers national learning and knowledge sharing activities for people involved in neighbourhood management, enhances knowledge about neighbourhood management and its potential to transform local services and reduce inequality and is a partner to government and national agencies.</p>
