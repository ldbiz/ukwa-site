<p>Street Level is a leading photography arts organisation that provides artists and the public with a range of opportunities to make and engage with photography.<br />
Digital Play : Wendy McMurdo Collected Works 1995 - 2012 is a major solo exhibition of collected works spanning the period from 1995 to 2012 by the photographer and filmmaker Wendy McMurdo. It is part of GENERATION, a celebration of Scottish contemporary art of the last 25 years to mark the Commonwealth Games in Glasgow.</p>
