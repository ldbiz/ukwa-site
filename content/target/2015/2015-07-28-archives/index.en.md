Archives+ is a City Centre showcase and repository for archives and family history. Situated in the magnificent Manchester Central Library, this purpose-built centre brings together a partnership of regional and national collections of documents, photographs and films, and helps to satisfy a growing demand for accessible community history and personal heritage.
