Ebrahim College (EC) is an institute of higher Islamic learning that aspires to operate as a fully-fledged university complete with a reputable research portfolio. It began in 2003 as an Islamic Seminary and an Islamic 6th Form, which, at the core of its academic offering, combined A levels with a programme called the Islamic Sciences Programme (ISP).
