The Royal Watercolour Society (RWS) was founded in 1804 and is an artist-led society. The members work in a variety of media including gouache, acrylic, pen and ink, pigment, collage, mixed media as well as traditional watercolour. The aim of the Society is to promote, by example and education, the understanding, appreciation and enjoyment of these media.
