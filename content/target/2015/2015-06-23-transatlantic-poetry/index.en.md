Transatlantic Poetry is a community of poets writing in (or translating to) English from the US, UK, Europe, and beyond. They host an innovative series of readings “on air” that brings poets together from across the globe using Google+ Hangouts on Air technology. The site is run by Richard Peak, a poet who lives in the UK.
