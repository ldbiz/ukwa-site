The group now comprises of the Birmingham Chamber of Commerce, Burton & District Chamber of Commerce, Chase Chamber of Commerce, Lichfield & Tamworth Chamber of Commerce, Solihull Chamber of Commerce; British American Business Council, Institute of Asian Businesses and Future Faces.
