The blog covers the 49 years Major Bob Smith spent with The Welch Regiment (later to become part of The Royal Regiment of Wales), The South Wales Borderers, King’s African Rifles and Malaysia Rangers. He became Curator of the Royal Regiment of Wales Museum (South Wales Borderers and Monmouthshire Regiment) and editor of the regimental magazine, ‘Men of Harlech’.
