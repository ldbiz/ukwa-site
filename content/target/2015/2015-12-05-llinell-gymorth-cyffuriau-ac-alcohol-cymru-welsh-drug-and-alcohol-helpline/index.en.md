The Wales Drug & Alcohol Helpline, also known as DAN 24/7 is managed by the Betsi Cadwaladr University Health Board.
DAN 24/7 provides a single point of contact for anyone in Wales wanting advice and / or help relating to drugs and / or alcohol. It is funded by the Welsh Government.
