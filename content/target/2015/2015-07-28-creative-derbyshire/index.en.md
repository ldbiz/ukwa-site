Creative Derbyshire is a programme of support and development for artists, arts organisations and creative businesses across Derbyshire. The focus is on developing the creative economy in Derbyshire and fostering a culture of innovation and artistic excellence. The programme is supported by Arts Derbyshire and Arts Council England.
