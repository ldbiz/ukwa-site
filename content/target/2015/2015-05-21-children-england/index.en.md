Children England is a membership organisation and collective voice for the children, young people and families voluntary sector.  They provide support to the sector through the membership package; promote good practice, networking and collective action; stimulate policy debate on the issues which matter most to their members; influence central government and other decision makers; deliver training, consultancy and support to the wider sector.
