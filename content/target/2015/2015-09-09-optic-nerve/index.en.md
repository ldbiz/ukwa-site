Optic Nerve Ltd is a London-based video production company which specialises in making documentaries on British and American poetry, and on contemporary music. The website includes excerpts from award-winning documentaries on American poets such as Allen Ginsberg, Amiri Baraka and Michael McClure, the acclaimed Channel 4 poetry series 'Arrows of Desire' and new music featuring, among others, the Kronos and Arditti string quartets. Much of this material has never been released and is only available on this site.
