Lactation Consultants of Great Britain (LCGB) is the professional association for International Board Certified Lactation Consultants (IBCLCs) in the UK, a professional voice for breastfeeding. LCGB promotes the work of IBCLCs and ensures mothers receive the help they need to breastfeed. LCGB also provides networking and learning opportunities for its members, including an annual conference.
