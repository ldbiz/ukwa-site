The International Association of Art Critics (AICA) brings together 4,200 art professionals from 70 countries all over the world, organised into 63 National Sections and an Open Section. It promotes art criticism as a discipline and contribute to its methodology; protects the ethical and professional interests of its members and co-operates in defending their rights; maintains an active international network for its members; contributes to mutual understanding of visual arts and aesthetics in all cultures; stimulates professional relationship across political, geographical, ethnic, economic and religious boundaries and defends impartially freedom of expression and thought and oppose arbitrary censorship.
