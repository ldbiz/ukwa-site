Founded in 1962, Young Enterprise and pfeg work directly with young people, their teachers and parents, businesses and influencers to build a successful and sustainable future for all young people and society at large. Through our hands-on employability and financial education programmes, resources and teacher training, they want to eradicate youth unemployment, help young people realise their potential beyond education and empower a generation to learn, to work and to live. Their mission is to empower young people to discover, develop and celebrate their skills and potential.
