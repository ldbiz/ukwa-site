EasySRE contains information about Sexual and Relationship Education. The website won the Pamela Sheridan Sex and Relationships Education Award in 2014. It contains a selection of eight films which address SRE concerns and was created using participatory video techniques with young people, teachers and health professionals to create high quality films which don't preach, but entertain and inform children and young people about relationships, sexual health, domestic abuse and well-being.
