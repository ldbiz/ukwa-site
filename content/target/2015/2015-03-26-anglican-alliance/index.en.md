Anglican Alliance - Development, Relief, Advocacy is an initiative of the Archbishop of Canterbury and the Anglican Communion. It is a platform for those across the Anglican Communion who work for a world free of poverty and injustice, to be a voice for the voiceless, to reconcile those in conflict, and to safeguard the earth. The website provide information on development, relief and advocacy projects.
