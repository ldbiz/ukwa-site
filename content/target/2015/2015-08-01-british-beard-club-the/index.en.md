The British Beard Club is a social meeting group for beard growers, their partners and families of The British Islands. They encourage, support and discuss the growing, maintenance and styling of beards and other facial hair. The British Beard Club is a non-political, non-orientated unincorporated charitable association of beard aficionados giving support, friendship, and advice to anyone who wants to grow a beard, has just started to grow theirs, or who has been growing a beard for years.
