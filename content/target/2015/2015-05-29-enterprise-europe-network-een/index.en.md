The Enterprise Europe Network provides free access to the information needed to strengthen businesses and form new collaborations across Europe. The network offers support and advice to businesses throughout Europe and assists you to capitalise on relevant opportunities in the European Union and beyond. Services are available to businesses, research centres and universities across Europe. The Enterprise Europe Network spans 51 countries and consists of nearly 600 partner organisations promoting competitiveness and innovation at a local level, in Europe and beyond.
