Great Britain Deaf Football has a very rich football history spanning back to 1924 where they participated in the First International Deaf Games in Paris 1924. Great Britain have since been crowned world champions six times, recently winning gold at the 2005 Deaflympics and is currently the most successful deaf footballing nation in the world.
