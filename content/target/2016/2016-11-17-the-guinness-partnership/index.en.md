The Guinness Partnership build and manage homes and housing services for around 120,000 customers. Of their 60,000 homes, more than 70% are let at a social or affordable rent, while around 10,000 are owned or part-owned by the people who live in them.
They also provide homes for older people and a range of care services, including care at home and extra care, supported and retirement living, delivering over 10,000 hours of care each week.
