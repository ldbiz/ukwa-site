Gambia-Swansea Vision 2020 Link is a partnership between The Sheik Zayed Regional Eye Care Centre, Banjul, The Gambia and Singleton Hospital Ophthalmology Department, Swansea. It is part of the World Health Organisation’s Vision 2020 project – a global initiative for the elimination of avoidable blindness.
