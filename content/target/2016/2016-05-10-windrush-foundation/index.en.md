The Windrush Foundation is is a registered Charity established in 1996. The organisation plays a role in preserving the history of the arrival of the first post war wave of Caribbean settlers at Tilbury Docks, Essex on 21 June 1948, and their contribution to modern Britain.

They receieved funding from the Heritage Lottery to conduct a project into the life and times of Dr James Risien Russell who was born in British Guiana in 1863, and who serviced Britain in World War as a Captain in the Royal Army Medical Corps.
