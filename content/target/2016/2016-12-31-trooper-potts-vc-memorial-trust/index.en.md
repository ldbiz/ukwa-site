The site was set up to assist us to raise funds to establish a memorial to the only man from Reading to be awarded the VC, this for his deeds in Gallipoli  21-23 August 1915.  The memorial was unveiled on 4 October 2015. The website shows the full story of Fred Potts and the man he rescued in youth and later life.  It also includes; information about our supporters and donors, the history of The Victoria Cross, the history of The Berkshire Yeomanry, timelines of the Great War, War Memorials and War Hospitals in Reading and the story of Reading.
