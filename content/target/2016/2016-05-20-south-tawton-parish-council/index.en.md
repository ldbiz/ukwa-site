The parish of South Tawton is situated to the north of Dartmoor and east of Okehampton. It sits partly within Dartmoor National Park and includes the communities of South Zeal, South Tawton, Taw Green, North Wyke and various other hamlets. It boasts some beautiful landscape, especially the magnificent Cawsand (or Cosdon!) Beacon. This website is run by South Tawton Parish Council, and features links to all the major activities of the Council.
