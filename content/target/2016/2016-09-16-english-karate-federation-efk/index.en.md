The English Karate Federation (EKF) is set-up as a democratically elected body for English Karate to cover the whole spectrum of Karate and to further develop an understanding administration that listens to the needs of all the many aspects of Karate from Sport to Traditional, from the youngest beginner to the most senior instructor.
