Founded in 1990, The Art Newspaper is an online and paper publication based in London and New York. It provides analysis about the art world, fed by its network of sister editions, which together have around 50 correspondents working in over 30 countries, with editorial offices in London, Turin, New York, Paris, Moscow, Beijing and Athens.
