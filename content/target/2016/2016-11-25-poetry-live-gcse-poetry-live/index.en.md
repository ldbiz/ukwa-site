Poetry Live! is a series of events around Britain featuring the poets included on the GCSE English Literature poetry syllabus, including Simon Armitage and Carol Anne Duffy.  It was founded by Simon Powell (1952-2009) so that students could hear the poetry they are studying read at live events.
