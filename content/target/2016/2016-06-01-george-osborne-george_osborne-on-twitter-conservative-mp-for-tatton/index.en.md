George Osborne was Member of Parliament for Tatton from June 2001 until he stood down on 3 May 2017. He served as Chancellor of the Exchequer under Prime Minister David Cameron from 2010 to 2016. In March 2017, he was appointed editor of the London Evening Standard, and started the role in May. He did not stand for re-election at the 2017 general election, having been a leader of the campaign for Britain to remain in the EU
