The Association of Residential Letting Agents (ARLA) is a professional body for individuals working in residential property lettings and management in the United Kingdom. ARLA was formed in 1981 and grew particularly as the private rented sector grew over the past 10 years. It a professional and regulatory body solely concerned with residential letting agents in the UK and is a part of the National Federation of Property Professionals (NFOPP).
