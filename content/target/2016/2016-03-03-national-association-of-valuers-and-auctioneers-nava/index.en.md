The National Association of Valuers and Auctioneers (NAVA) is a professional self regulating body solely concerned with valuers and auctioneers. Founded in 1988 on the principles of expertise, experience, integrity and honesty, NAVA represents over 300 valuers and auctioneers; providing a focal point for its widespread membership throughout the UK.
