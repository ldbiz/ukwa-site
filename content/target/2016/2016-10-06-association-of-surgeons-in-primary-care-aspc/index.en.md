The Association of Surgeons in Primary Care (ASPC) is affiliated with the Association of Surgeons of Great Britain and Ireland and has links with the Royal College of General Practitioners, the Association of Surgeons in Training and the British Association of day surgery. From February 2013 the ASPC affiliated with the PCSA, their sister organisation in the Republic of Ireland.
