This a four-year AHRC-funded research project jointly run by the University of Wales Centre for Advanced Welsh and Celtic Studies (CAWCS) and the University of Glasgow. The project explores Romantic-period accounts of journeys into Wales and Scotland. The focus will be on the writings of the Flintshire naturalist and antiquarian Thomas Pennant (1726-1798).
