Coca-Cola HBC AG (Coca‑Cola Hellenic Bottling Company) is a bottling partner of The Coca‑Cola Company. Whereby The Coca‑Cola Company manufactures and sells concentrates, bases and syrups to its bottling partners, owns the brands and is responsible for consumer brand marketing. It is the world's second largest Coca-Cola anchor bottler in terms of volume with sales of more than 2 billion unit cases.
