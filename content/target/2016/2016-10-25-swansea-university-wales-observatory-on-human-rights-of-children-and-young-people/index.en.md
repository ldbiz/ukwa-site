The Wales Observatory on Human Rights of Children and Young People, based at Swansea University, was established in 2012. The Observatory 'provides a forum for research, advocacy and expertise on human rights of children and young people and supports public policy, practice, child advocacy and law reform conducive to implementation of human rights obligations'.
