Seb Dance stood as a Labour candidate for London at the 2014 European Parliament election and was elected to serve as an MEP.  On entering the European Parliament, he was appointed to the European Parliament Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety, responsible for a wide range of policy areas including air and water pollution, waste management, and climate change. He is strongly in favour of remaining in the EU.
