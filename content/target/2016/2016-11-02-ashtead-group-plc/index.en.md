Ashtead is an international equipment rental company with national networks in the US and the UK and a small presence in Canada. They rent a full range of construction and industrial equipment across a wide variety of applications to a diverse customer base. Their equipment can be used to lift, power, generate, move, dig, compact, drill, support, scrub, pump, direct, heat and ventilate.
