The Wales and the EU Hub is a project created to provide and disseminate non-partisan and independent research on Wales and the EU and is funded by the Economic and Social Research Council’s The UK in a Changing Europe initiative. The Wales and the EU Hub is part of the Wales Governance Centre and is supported by the Centre for European Law and Governance at Cardiff University’s School of Law and Politics.
