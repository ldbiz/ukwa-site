The Centre for Sustainable Aquaculture Research that is dedicated to developing and transferring sustainable aquaculture solutions through advocacy, education, training and consultancy services. The Centre for Sustainable Aquatic Research, CSAR, is a centre of excellence founded in 2003 with support from the European Union, Welsh Government, and Swansea University.
