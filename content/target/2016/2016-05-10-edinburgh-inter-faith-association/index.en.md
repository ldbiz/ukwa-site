Site of an association of different religious groups aiming to promote religious harmony, diversity and equal opportunities, in Edinburgh and its wider environs by bringing together the faith communities in deeper dialogue which leads towards mutual understanding, trust, respect, co-operation and peace, to advance education of the general public on inter-faith and multicultural issues to further their awareness and understanding, contribute toward the development of a global culture of peace by working both locally and globally to improve co-operation between faiths and cultures.
