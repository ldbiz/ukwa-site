Established in 1818 and with over 85,000 members
worldwide, ICE is a leading source of expertise in
infrastructure and engineering policy and is widely seen
as the independent voice of infrastructure. ICE provides
advice to all political parties and works with industry to
ensure that civil engineering and construction remain
major contributors to the UK economy.
