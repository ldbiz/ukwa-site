<p>Ken Frost runs a well known network of websites under the "Living Brand" label (<a href="http://www.kenfrost.com">www.kenfrost.com</a>).<br />

He was also ranked 11th by Accountancy Age in their 2006 Financial Power List of the most influential people in the world of finance.
